    Steps

    Prototype v1. Data is saved locally in this browser only. No backend, no patient privacy risk.

    Personal History

    Build the opening paragraph: identity, demographics, occupation, residence, marital data, special habits, and conditional female/neuro items.

    NASOMRH engine
    Ask like a clinician, not a robot: “What is your name? How old are you? What do you do? Where do you live?” Then convert it into a clean paragraph.
    Shown in Neurology mode.
    0Smoking index
    0Pack-years

    Female / menstrual history

    Complaint

    Keep it short: patient’s own words, main complaint, and duration. This becomes your C/O line.

    C/O + duration
    Complaint should not be over-medicalized. Write what brought the patient, then summarize it clinically in one line.

    History of Present Illness

    Analyze the complaint, then build a chronological story with related symptoms and prior management.

    Story builder
    Core analysis: onset, course, duration, associations, aggravating/relieving factors, effect of treatment, and last attack. Pain/excreta get extra fields.

    Related System Symptoms

    Checklist changes according to the selected system. Use notes to add positives and important negatives.

    General
    In OSCE, related symptoms are where you prove you understand the system, not just the chief complaint.

    Past History

    Diseases, operations, transfusion, drug history, allergies, and chronic risk factors like DM/HTN/TB/IHD.

    Disease · Operative · Drugs
    For any past disease: ask duration, manifestations, investigations, treatment, and complications.

    Family + Social History

    Consanguinity, similar conditions, chronic diseases, living conditions, and occupational clues.
